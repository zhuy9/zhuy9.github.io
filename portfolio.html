  • Planet Farm

    Planet Farm is an interactive app that allows you to add different kinds of animals to your farm. These animals will move around randomly. If one predator collides with its prey, the prey will disappear. Besides animals, you can also add fences, or grasses to the farm.

  • Automatic Checklist

    Automatic Checklist is an android app to help user stay organized. For the conventional web-based or application-based checklist apps, I have to open a website or the app to view or edit my entries. With Automatic Checklist, the user can view the events directly in the calendar. Each entry in the app will have an optional label (Work, study, family, etc), an optional deadline, and an optional location. Besides viewing all the entries, the user can also view them by categories since the app can filter the entries by the labels.
